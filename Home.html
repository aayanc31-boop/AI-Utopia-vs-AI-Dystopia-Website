What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the science of creating machines and software that can perform tasks normally requiring human intelligence. This includes learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and recognizing patterns. AI is transforming industries, education, and healthcare, but it also raises important ethical and social questions about how it should be used responsibly.

Why This Matters

The decisions we make today about AI development and implementation will define tomorrow's world. By exploring both the promise and peril of artificial intelligence, we can work toward a future that amplifies human potential while safeguarding our values, autonomy, and wellbeing. Your voice in this conversation matters—every choice you make here reflects the larger choices our society faces.
