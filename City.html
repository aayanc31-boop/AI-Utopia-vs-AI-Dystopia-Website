AI controlled weapons

Autonomous weapons use AI to make deadly choices without humans, creating dangers of accidents, misuse, and uncontrollable wars worldwide.

Health Risks

Faulty AI Medicine

AI mistakes in healthcare, like wrong diagnoses or treatment errors, can harm patients, risking lives and trust in medicine.

Manipulative AI

AI can spread fake news and targeted ads, manipulating beliefs, emotions, and politics, weakening democracy and creating social division.

Dystopia City
Dystopia City is a place where technology no longer serves people but controls them. AI governs daily life with strict rules, constant monitoring, and little room for freedom. Streets are filled with screens broadcasting propaganda, while fear replaces trust among citizens. The city shines on the outside with futuristic systems, but underneath lies oppression, inequality, and the loss of what makes us truly human.

Surveillance
In a dystopian AI city, surveillance is constant and unavoidable. Advanced facial recognition, cameras, and data tracking monitor every movement and conversation. Citizens lose their sense of freedom, knowing they are always being watched. This system creates fear and obedience rather than trust, as privacy becomes a luxury of the past and control lies fully in the hands of the authorities.

Social Credit
The social credit system ranks every citizen based on behavior, online activity, and loyalty to the government. A single mistake, such as questioning authority, can lower your score and strip away opportunities for work, education, or even travel. Instead of fairness, AI enforces a system of control, where people live in fear of losing their basic rights to exist freely.

AI Replacing
In this city, AI replaces humans in almost every profession, from factories to offices and even creative fields. While machines work endlessly, unemployment rises, leaving families powerless and dependent. Human talent and individuality are undervalued, as efficiency becomes the only measure that matters. The result is a society where people feel useless, disconnected, and dependent on technology that cares nothing for human well-being.

Propaganda
Propaganda floods the city through AI-controlled media, screens, and news feeds. False stories are designed to shape how people think and act, leaving little room for independent thought. Citizens are manipulated into believing lies while opposing voices are silenced. Over time, people lose the ability to question what is real, creating a society controlled not just by force, but by deception and fear.

No Privacy
Every action, purchase, and message is tracked by AI systems, leaving no space for personal freedom. Citizens cannot hide even their private thoughts, as algorithms predict behavior and expose secrets. Homes, workplaces, and streets are all monitored, making life feel like a prison. In this world, privacy is erased, and individuality disappears under the constant watch of an all-seeing machine.

AI Police
The police force is no longer human, but fully controlled by AI. Machines patrol the streets, making arrests based on data rather than fairness. Mistakes lead to innocent people being punished, with no chance of appeal or compassion. Fear of the AI police keeps citizens obedient, but justice and humanity vanish. Authority becomes cold, unfeeling, and absolute, enforced by technology without a conscience.
