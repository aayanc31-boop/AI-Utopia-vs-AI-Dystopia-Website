Types of AI Ethics

Algorithmic Bias

Addressing bias in AI algorithms to ensure fair and equitable outcomes.

Data Privacy

Protecting sensitive user data and ensuring responsible data handling practices.

Transparency & Explainability

Making AI decision-making processes transparent and understandable.

AI Safety

Developing AI systems that are safe, reliable, and aligned with human values.
